Who is Stash?

Stash is a South African born RnB/Soul singer, songwriter and guitarist.Stash's heartfelt lyrics and passionate vocals bring forth a honest delivery often compared to Micheal Buble, Lucky Day, John Legend and Ed Sheeran. He performs his music from a very personal and sincere perspective, crafting engaging stories and melodies. Stash has played at live venues such as Coco De Mar, Cubana and Finezz.


Career highlights include working with Rude World Record artists Coppinville and Half Tiger. He has also been interview on Gheberha community television station Bay TV and community radio station Bay FM. Stash finds his musical inspiration from artists like James Arthur, Stevie Wonder, Tems and Kings of Leon amongst others. His single "Just Like You" is due for release on the 03 December 2022, the song highlights his accoustic roots with a relatable story line and catchy chorus.
